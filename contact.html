Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?
We deliver Monday through Sunday from 8 AM to 8 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time through our mobile app or website dashboard. You'll also receive SMS updates.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any product, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund.
Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and business customers. Contact our sales team for more information.
